Funding

PhysioHead is open to project-based support from independent organizations. As governance and project pipelines mature, this section can be expanded according to process needs and strategic priorities.

Current approach

  • Support is considered on a project-by-project basis.
  • Priority is given to scientific independence and transparency.
  • Funding scope will grow in line with SIG development milestones.

Next phase

Detailed funding streams, governance safeguards, and reporting templates will be published as the SIG process evolves.
