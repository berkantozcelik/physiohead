Independent collaboration model

Funding & Strategic Partnerships

PhysioHead operates independently to advance non-pharmacological, physiotherapy, and behavioral approaches in global headache care. We build strategic partnerships to support high-impact science, technology-enabled care, and educational infrastructure across international headache pathways.

Our Funding Pillars

We are building support around three practical pathways: innovation, research productivity, and educational capacity.

Industry & Innovation Integration Global Research Productivity Educational Infrastructure
Pillar 01

Industry & Innovation Integration

Partnering with digital health and tele-rehabilitation developers to adapt technology into clinical practice and open new pathways for rehabilitation professionals.

Pillar 02

Global Research Productivity

Supporting multicenter clinical research, international exchange, and publication pathways that strengthen high-quality academic output across regions.

Pillar 03

Educational Infrastructure

Funding guidance development, masterclasses, and scalable training models that equip the next generation of clinicians.

Current approach

Project-Based Support With Clear Safeguards

All support is evaluated on a project-by-project basis with strict commitment to scientific independence, transparency, and multidisciplinary integrity. Core operations are sustained through our collaborative network while funding scope expands in line with strategic milestones.

Scientific independence Transparent review Multidisciplinary integrity
