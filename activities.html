Planned Activities

PhysioHead will convene multidisciplinary expertise through meetings, publications, education, and public engagement to elevate non-pharmacological headache care.

Scientific meetings

  • Virtual SIG meetings
  • Symposia and workshops at international congresses
  • Focused sessions on physiotherapy and behavioral care

Academic outputs

  • White paper on non-pharmacological headache management
  • Narrative and systematic reviews
  • Multicenter research initiatives

Educational initiatives

  • Educational resources for clinicians
  • Cross-disciplinary training materials
  • Integration into headache education curricula

Public and patient engagement

  • Public awareness campaigns
  • Patient-centered educational materials
  • Contributions to World Headache Awareness initiatives

Expected outcomes & impact

How the SIG translates activities into measurable change.

Visibility & credibility

Improved recognition of physiotherapy and behavioral headache care.

Multidisciplinary collaboration

Stronger networks across neurology, physiotherapy, psychology, and allied health.

Evidence-informed guidance

Consensus recommendations to support clinical practice worldwide.

Expanded access

Greater access to comprehensive non-pharmacological care, including telehealth.

Global strategy

Strengthened regional and international headache care strategies.

Future-ready

PhysioHead is designed as a sustainable SIG with regular leadership review, ongoing recruitment, and integration with existing education and research initiatives.
