Physiotherapy & Behavioral Approaches in Headache Care.

PhysioHead is a proposed Special Interest Group (SIG) dedicated to evidence-based physiotherapy, behavioral, and digital non-pharmacological approaches to headache management. We connect clinicians, researchers, and educators to elevate care beyond pharmacology.

Explore the mission See planned work
Why this SIG

Addressing the care gap in headache management.

Headache disorders remain a major global burden. Many people experience limited response, intolerance, or preference to avoid medication. PhysioHead creates a structured, international platform to standardize, research, and disseminate non-pharmacological care.
